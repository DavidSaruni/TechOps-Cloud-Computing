Coffee Beans Hub named cradle of the Silicon Savannah

13th January, 2023. Article by Jackson Martin & Dr. Mwendo Juma

Following the annual Afri-cities conference that occured this year in Kenya. Coffee Beans Hub was recognizied as one of the most uniques, culture-based and successful tech-startups. CBH, Nairobi emerged a champion amongst other big sharks in the industry. The award proved that the community in Nairobi was strongly growing and the strategy of collaboration that CBH had employed was beginning to to show its relevance. CBH has trained over 1000+ new developer over the past 8 months since it was started and has now grown to be one of the loved choices when employers look for developers.
